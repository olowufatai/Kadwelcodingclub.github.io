About Us

We are Kadwel primary coding club, we are dedicated to impact IT knowledge to our brand ambassadors, coding club is one of the top nortch club in the school, we have proven these by engaging/exposing our pupils to the 24th century digital world. The world is moving to a global relm, which we want our people not to be left behind. In coding club we divide our class into session, we have the lower primary and the upper primary, we engage the lower primary section basic 1-3 on Scratch programming language, which will enable them to build an interactive games and so on, we engage the upper primary section basic 4-6 on how to build a productive website.
